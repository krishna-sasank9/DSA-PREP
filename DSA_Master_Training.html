📅 Daily Study Plan
1

Morning (30 min) — Theory

Read the topic theory, understand the pattern, write key notes in your solutions/topic/notes.md

2

Afternoon (60–90 min) — Solve

Solve 2–3 Easy + 1–2 Medium questions. Time yourself: Easy ≤ 15 min, Medium ≤ 30 min, Hard ≤ 45 min.

3

Evening (20 min) — Review

Re-solve failed problems without looking. Push solutions to GitHub with meaningful commit messages.

4

Weekly (Sunday)

Mock interview: pick 1 Easy + 2 Medium from a company section. Solve in 90 min with no hints. Update README.md stats.

📌 Commit Message Convention

✅ [Topic] Problem Name - Approach used - Time/Space
Example: ✅ [Graphs] Number of Islands - BFS - O(m*n)
